GvOS

A free and stylized OS. Based on LineageOS.

About

GvOS is a free operating system, and open source! It is based on LineageOS, with (experimental) ability to use Android and Linux apps!
GvOS focuses on customization and having a smooth experience, with preinstalled useful apps, for media, gaming, and browsing.
