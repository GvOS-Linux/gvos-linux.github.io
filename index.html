GvOS

A free and stylized OS that looks like Windows.

About

GvOS is a free operating system, and open source! It is based on Debian with a look and feel of Windows 11.
GvOS focuses on customization and having a smooth experience, with preinstalled useful apps, for media, gaming, and browsing.
