Welcome to GvOS

GvOS is a free operating system built on the principles of freedom, reliability, and community collaboration.

About GvOS

Free Software

GvOS is completely free and will always remain free. The system is developed by a community of volunteers dedicated to creating the best free operating system.

Stable & Reliable

Built with stability in mind, GvOS provides a solid foundation for servers, desktops, and embedded systems.

Community Driven

Join thousands of contributors and users who make GvOS what it is. Everyone is welcome to participate and contribute.
