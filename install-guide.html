How to Install GvOS

Step 1: Download the GvOS ISO from the official website.

Step 2: Create a bootable USB using tools like Rufus (Windows) or dd (Linux/macOS). Or, you could use GvOS in a VM.

Step 3: Boot your computer using the USB drive in your BIOS device boot. Let it boot, and

Step 4: Follow the guided instructions for selecting your language, setting up partitions, and installing the OS.

Step 5: Reboot and enjoy your new GvOS installation!
